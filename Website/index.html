Elderly Integrated Care Device

UCSC HACKATHON 2015

Hello, we are Team CSUMB coming from California State University, Monterey Bay. We have developed a concept using the Plantronics WC1 Headset and the Tessel which can aid assisted living facilities in monitoring a variety of different health and safety related emergencies. Our demonstration showcases only a few of the major features and capabilities of our idea which we believe can be highly advanced upon and marketed towards all assisted living facilities and even the private sector.
